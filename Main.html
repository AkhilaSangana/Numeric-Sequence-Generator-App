For larger numbers, result set is large and you could scroll down the page using mouse to see the results or collapse the sections by clicking on the header.
Custom numbers panel shows numbers multiples of 3 with C,5 with E, 3 & 5 with Z.
You could collapse this instructions list by clicking instructions button again.
